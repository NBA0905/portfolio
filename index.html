About me

I'm a student software developer based in the Netherlands. I started with an internship building production iOS software, and liked it enough that they asked me back — so now I split my time between studying and shipping real features used by real people.

Lately I've been most excited about emerging hardware: I've been prototyping voice-driven experiences for smart glasses, digging into Bluetooth audio sessions, speech-to-text pipelines, and how apps should behave when there's no screen to tap.

I care about code that's easy to read six months later, and products that feel obvious to use on the first try.
